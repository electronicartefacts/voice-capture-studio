# Code Of Conduct

Electronic Artefacts projects are maintained for clear, careful technical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume technical disagreement is normal and resolve it with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Credit ideas, prior work, and reported issues accurately.
- Respect privacy, especially around voice data and identity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information or user data.
- Dismissing security, privacy, accessibility, or safety concerns without review.
- Repeated off-topic disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or decline
contributions that violate this code. If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the
maintainers through the repository's private channels when available.
